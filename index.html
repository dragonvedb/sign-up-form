UNVEINED

Unveined is a new kind of online social blogging space.

A free, open-source platform, designed from the ground up to encourage deep, thougtful conversations, reward well-researched content, promote inter-disciplinary cross-polination, as well as allowing communities to create shared knowledge databases and discussion archives instead of rehashing same old "discourse" ad nauseam.

It is, first and foremost, a vehicle for collective understanding.
